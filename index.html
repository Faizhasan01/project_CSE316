Disk Scheduling
Basic Information

Disk scheduling is the process of managing and optimizing the order in which disk I/O requests are serviced. Since the mechanical movement of the disk's read/write head takes time, efficient scheduling helps reduce seek time, improves system throughput, and enhances overall performance..
